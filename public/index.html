Waste Less Live More

WHAT IS THIS?

WLLM is an idea. The idea is that it doesn't have to be that hard to have a happy, healthy life that doesn't fuck anyone else over. We're so used to the idea of tradeoffs, of giving something up in order to benefit in another way and in some cases, that's a reality. However, so many of the lifestyle changes that I've made in my own life have been win-win-win situations.
